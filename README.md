## 📖 梅花易数简介 | About Plum Blossom Numerology

梅花易数是宋代易学大师**邵雍**（邵康节）所创的占卜方法。其核心理念是"万物皆数"——世间一切事物皆可用数来表达，通过数字转化为八卦卦象，再依据卦象之间的五行生克关系来推断事物的吉凶。

*Plum Blossom Yi Shu* was created by **Shao Yong**, a master of Yi-ology in the Song Dynasty (960-1279). Its core philosophy is that "all things are numbers" — everything in the world can be expressed through numbers, which are then converted into trigrams of the I Ching. The interplay of Five Elements (Wu Xing) between trigrams reveals the fortune of the matter in question.
